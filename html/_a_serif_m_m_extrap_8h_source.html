20 /* Adobe Serif MM base font extrapolation limits aggregate initializer. Element
21  values are an array of 8 extrapolation limits. Index by glyph index, get
22  extrapolation limit data.
23 
24  Extapolation limits are relative to the unit em square and describe an
25  extrapolation into space to the left, bottom, right, and top of the square.
26 
27  In each glyph, the first pair of extrapolation limits bound the left
28  extrapolation: lower-left-x and upper-left-x. The next pair bound the right
29  extrapolation: upper-right-x and lower-right-x. The next pair bound the
30  bottom extrapolation: lower-left-y and lower-right-y. And finally, the next
31  pair bound the top extrapolation: upper-right-y and upper-left-y.
32 
33  All the extrapolation values are in em units and are represented in 6.10
34  fixed point format.
35 
36  The lower-left-x, upper-left-x, lower-left-y, and lower-right-y values are
