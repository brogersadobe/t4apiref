Detailed Description

ContentRangeReflowLayout instances keep track of a content range and its associated reflow transform. They are constructed by and stored on a ReflowLayout instance, and are updated by a LayoutEngine subclass when the reflowed view is calculated. They persist beyond the reflow calculation and their information is used at draw time to draw content at the reflowed locations.
