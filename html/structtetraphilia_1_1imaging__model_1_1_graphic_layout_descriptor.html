◆ Interleaving

enum tetraphilia::imaging_model::GraphicLayoutDescriptor::Interleaving

Describes the way the various signals in a GraphicStore are interleaved.

Enumerator
kChunky 

All signals in every channel (shape, opacity, color) are interleaved. Example:

fqRGBfqRGBfqRGB...

kChannelPlanes 

Signals within each channel are interleaved, but individual channels are separate. Example:

ffffff... qqqqqq... RGBRGBRGB....

kSignalPlanes 

Every signal gets its own plane. Example:

ffffffff... qqqqqqqq... RRRRRRRR... GGGGGGGG... BBBBBBBB...
